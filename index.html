Frequently Asked Questions

What is Status Symbol Empire?

We are a community of people who aspire to be the greatest version of ourselves. Always seeking knowledge and growth, spiritually, mentally, physically, and financially. Our team consists of Entrepreneurs, Life Coaches, Fitness Trainers/Nutritionists, Real Estate Agents, and Investors (Stocks/Cryptocurrency).

Why are you doing this for free?

In this day age, information is free. You can find anything you want on the internet, and can be self-taught. The issue is that people don't know what to look for and how to execute. While all of our courses will be free, we do offer a premium (paid) package with exclusive benefits/courses and mentorship.

Do you offer premium content?

Yes we do! We wanted to make sure that some of our most consistent and determined community members can have access to exclusive benefits inside of the SSE family. As of right now, our plan is to have a Discord Community for 1 on 1 mentorship and live discussions, a referral system that rewards you, and so much more!

Do I have to have prior experience?

Status Symbol Empire is beginner friendly, the goal is to always keep evolving as people. So even if you have tons of experience we are sure that our team members will be able to enhance your tool kit in some way shape or form. Remember, you choose what areas you wish to evolve and take the courses that you want!

How often can we expect new material?

At launch we will have 3 courses available about investing for you to get started on. The plan is to have a new course every 3 months, the reason for this is so people have adequate time to complete the previous course and get any questions answered or mentorship they need on those specific topics. Don't worry, we will keep you busy!

Is this open source, can we contribute?

As of right now, we are keeping things in-house until we have an established flow with our current team members. What we can guarantee is that we are looking to quickly adapt and grow the number of courses and experts that we have on staff. So if you are interested, I would continue reaching out and contributing where you can, inside of the community!

Do I have to sign-up with a social media account?

No you do not. But its highly suggested as we will be doing giveaways through social media that will make it easier for us to verify following, and that our posts are being engaged. We also will be using a service called "Gleam" that manages giveaways which will have ticket submissions that require you to link social medias, etc. It is highly recommended that you connect an account.

Can I get a refund if I don't like the course? or if I change my mind?

Our courses are free, so no refunds apply there. As far as Premium Package members go, we are not just approving anyone for this package. The reason that we have this as an option is for people who are serious about taking charge of their lives and creating generational wealth through hard work and great mentors. No refunds on this purchase!
